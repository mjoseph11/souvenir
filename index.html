History

SGMOC Golden Jubilee • 1976–2026

Our Story

Five Decades of Faith • Fellowship • Service

St. Gregorios Malankara Orthodox Church, Bensalem, Pennsylvania stands as one of the earliest parishes established by the Malankara Orthodox Church in the American Diaspora. The parish was founded in 1976 by a small but faithful community of Malayali Orthodox Christians who had recently emigrated to the Philadelphia area, driven by their deep desire to continue the ancient liturgical traditions of the Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church of India.

The early years were marked by humble gatherings in homes and borrowed halls. The founders, guided by a strong sense of faith and the blessings of the Diocese, laid the spiritual and organizational foundations that would carry the church through the decades.

The consecration of the church building was a landmark moment in the parish's history, providing a permanent spiritual home for the growing congregation. The sanctuary was dedicated in the name of St. Gregorios of Parumala — the first Indian saint of the Orthodox Church — whose intercessory prayers have guided the parish through the years.

Under the pastoral guidance of successive vicars, the parish grew in membership and ministry. Sunday School, youth ministries, women's organizations, and charitable services were established to serve both the community and those beyond the church walls.

Today, SGMOC is a vibrant congregation that proudly carries forward the apostolic tradition of the Malankara Orthodox Church — offering the ancient Holy Qurbana in the West Syriac rite, nurturing the faith of its families, and reaching out with love to all in need.

As we celebrate fifty years of God's faithfulness, we remember with gratitude all the clergy, lay leaders, and faithful members who have served, sacrificed, and prayed to build this beloved parish. To God be the glory.

Our Ministries

Serving God • Serving Community

Sunday School

Nurturing the next generation in the Orthodox faith through scripture, liturgy, and the rich traditions of the Malankara Church. Our Sunday School has been the spiritual foundation for hundreds of children over five decades.

MGOCSM — Youth League

Malankara Orthodox Christian Student Movement brings together the youth of the parish for prayer, fellowship, service, and retreats. A vibrant community of young Orthodox Christians growing in faith and leadership.

STG — Serve Transform Grow

A dynamic ministry focused on community outreach, spiritual transformation, and growing disciples. STG organizes service projects, Bible study, and programs that equip members to live out their faith.

GROW — Women's Ministry

Empowering women of faith through prayer, study, and service. GROW brings women together to deepen their relationship with God and with one another, reflecting the grace and strength of Orthodox Christian womanhood.

Marth Mariam Samajam

The Mary Martha Society — a treasured women's organization of the Malankara Orthodox Church dedicated to prayer, charitable service, and the spiritual formation of its members in the tradition of the Church Mothers.

Good Samaritan

Our charitable ministry reaching out beyond the parish walls — supporting the poor, the sick, and those in need. Inspired by the parable of the Good Samaritan, this ministry is the compassionate heart of our congregation.
